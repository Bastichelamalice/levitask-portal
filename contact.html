Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place my first order?
Contact Nicolas directly at nicolas@levitask.com with your desired quantities and delivery address. He will confirm availability, lead times, and pricing. You can also download the full Order Process guide in Resources & Downloads → Legal & Operations.
What are current lead times?
Lead times vary by region and order size. As a general guideline, standard orders ship within 4–6 weeks. For up-to-date lead time information, contact Nico or refer to the Order Process document in Resources & Downloads.
Can I use Levitask branding and assets in my own marketing?
Yes: within the guidelines defined in the Brand Guidelines document available in Resources & Downloads. All usage must respect the approved color palette, logo rules, and tone of voice. If in doubt, contact Bastien before publishing.
A customer wants to return a chair: what do I do?
First, refer to the Returns & Exchanges Policy in Resources & Downloads to check eligibility. Then contact Nico to initiate the return process. Do not accept a return without confirming with Levitask first: this protects both you and the customer.
How do I request a demo unit?
Demo units are available to active distributors. Contact Nico with your shipping address and the model(s) you need. Demo units are provided at a preferential rate and are expected to be used for customer-facing demonstrations.
I've completed my training: how do I get my certification?
Once you've marked all 5 training modules as complete, the Certification Assessment will unlock in the Training section. Complete the knowledge quiz with a passing score and your Levitask Certified Distributor certificate will be generated automatically. Questions? Contact Bastien.
How do I get access to new portal features or updated assets?
The portal is updated regularly. New assets are flagged with a "New" badge in Resources & Downloads, and your Dashboard will show the latest updates each time you log in. For urgent requests or custom assets, contact Bastien.
